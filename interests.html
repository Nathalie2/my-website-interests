My Interests in Cybersecurity

  • Network Security: Protecting networks from breaches and monitoring for potential threats.
  • Ethical Hacking: Identifying vulnerabilities in systems by simulating attacks to strengthen defenses.
  • Data Privacy: Ensuring that sensitive information is stored securely and that privacy policies are upheld.
  • Incident Response: Managing and mitigating cyberattacks, restoring services, and learning from security breaches.
  • Malware Analysis: Studying malicious software to understand its structure and develop countermeasures.
  • Cryptography: The science of protecting information by transforming it into secure formats.
